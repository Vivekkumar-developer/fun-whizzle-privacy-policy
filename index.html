Privacy Policy – Fun Whizzle

Effective Date: May 9, 2026 Last Updated: June 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fun Whizzle ("we", "our", or "us"). Fun Whizzle is a children's educational mobile application designed to make learning fun through interactive games covering words, numbers, shapes, colors, and basic mathematics.

We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

By downloading or using Fun Whizzle, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Who This App Is For

Fun Whizzle is designed for children of all ages as an educational learning tool. Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise their children's use of the app and review this policy carefully.

We comply with applicable children's privacy laws including:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act – USA)
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation – EU/UK)
  • DPDP Act (Digital Personal Data Protection Act – India)

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

Fun Whizzle is designed to be a fully offline-capable learning app. We do not collect:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
  • Location data (GPS or IP-based)
  • Photos, videos, or microphone recordings stored outside the device
  • Social media profiles or login credentials
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII) from children

3.2 Information Stored Locally on Device

All game progress is stored only on your device using local storage (SharedPreferences). This includes:

  • Game level progress
  • Star ratings earned per level
  • Selected difficulty and age group settings
  • Audio and display preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

3.3 Audio (Text-to-Speech)

Fun Whizzle uses your device's built-in Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine to pronounce words and letters aloud during gameplay. This feature:

  • Uses the TTS engine already installed on your device
  • Does not record or store any audio
  • Does not send any audio data to external servers

4. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use, sell, rent, share, or disclose any personal data about our users.

Game progress stored locally on the device is used solely to:

  • Resume gameplay from where the child left off
  • Display earned stars and unlocked levels
  • Personalise the difficulty experience

5. Third-Party Services

Fun Whizzle may use the following third-party services:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Google Fonts Display child-friendly typography None – fonts are bundled locally
Flaticon / CDN image assets Display educational images (animals, objects) None – images are loaded by URL at runtime

We do not use:

  • Advertising networks or SDKs
  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Social login services
  • In-app purchase tracking

6. Advertising

Fun Whizzle contains no advertisements. We do not display ads, track ad performance, or share any data with advertising networks.

7. In-App Purchases

Fun Whizzle does not currently offer in-app purchases. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated and parents will be notified through the app store listing.

8. Permissions We Request

Depending on your device, Fun Whizzle may request the following permissions:

Permission Why We Need It
Audio / Text-to-Speech To speak words and letters aloud during word games
Internet To load educational images from our CDN (no personal data is sent)
Vibration To provide gentle haptic feedback when correct answers are given

We do not request permission to access:

  • Camera or Microphone (for recording)
  • Contacts or Call logs
  • Location services
  • External storage beyond app-specific files

9. Parental Controls and Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review all data stored by the app (located in device settings → App Storage)
  • Delete all app data at any time by clearing the app's storage in device settings or uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of any future data collection by contacting us (see Section 12)

To delete all local game data:

  1. Go to your device Settings
  2. Select Apps → Fun Whizzle
  3. Tap Clear Data / Clear Storage

10. Data Security

Although we do not collect personal data, we take the following steps to protect locally stored data:

  • All game data is stored in the device's sandboxed app storage, inaccessible to other apps
  • We do not transmit any game data over the internet
  • We do not maintain servers or databases containing user information

  • Significant changes will be communicated through an in-app notice or an updated app store description

We encourage parents to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
